Botch reunion when?

how did we get here?

On June 15th, 2026 the band performed for the last time at The Showbox in Seattle, Wa. The performance was filmed and later released on cd and dvd titled 061502 on December 5, 2006, ending their discography which studio albums consists of

  1. American Nervoso (1998)
  2. We Are the Romans (1999)
  3. An Anthology of Dead Ends (2002)

The final line-up of the band at the time of its death were

  • Dave Verellen – vocals
  • Dave Knudson – guitar
  • Brian Cook – bass guitar
  • Tim Latona – drums

where are we?

in 2020 the bands once record label went out of business which resulted in the bands entire discography being completely purged from all music streaming services until October 2021, when the Sargent House Records announced that they had acquired Botch's full catalogue and would "maintain the band's digital presence as well as future vinyl represses in the future" which created speculation amongst the fanbase for a future reunion. Each member went on to say that this was purely just going to be for the purpose of keeping their records in print and that "you shouldn't expect any new music from us." until August 24, 2022 when Botch released the single “One Twenty Two”
